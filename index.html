The story of Your Princess Pal

Here at Your Princess Pal our moto is fairytales for every budget. Our prices are competitive & all our magic is brought to you on behalf of performer’s trained in the art of musical theatre to help bring these beloved characters to life. Your Princess Pal visits events and parties for all ages over the Ottawa & Gatineau area.
